Play With the Expedians


Expedians United organize weekly football practices at different fields in Prague. This is for all an opportunity to join an open, inclusive and diverse group of people to enjoy regular football practices


There is also the possibility to then join one of the Expedians United teams and compete in one of the leagues we are engaged in. The conditions for joining one of our teams are to fit with our philosophy as well as with a desired football level. Both are regularly assessed by the management team who will reach out to you if they believe we are a match.


Joining or not one of the teams is not a condition to come play with us regularly. We are also a community of more than 200 players who regularly meet and play together, most of those not being part of the competitive teams.


Join our group on Meetup and sign in to any of our weekly practice sessions.
